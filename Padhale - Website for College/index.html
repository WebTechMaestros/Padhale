About Us

Welcome to Padhle, your ultimate destination for collaborative learning and academic excellence!

Frequently Asked Questions

Explore our Frequently Asked Questions to find answers to common queries about our platform. We're here to provide clarity and support to enhance your experience. If you don't find what you're looking for, feel free to reach out to us for personalized assistance

  • Kya tere notes hi kaafi hai UTs aur End-Sem k liye?

    Yes...Of course yaar, jo professors class me padhate mai wohi sb rough me likhta hu aur fir ghar aake use acche se organized tarike se likhta hu, aur agar lage toh usme kuch cheeje add ya subtract krta hu.

  • Notes kaha se download kaha se krna bhaiyaa?

    Just go to Features and then to Handwritten Notes section and then select the ones which you wanna download.

  • Can I Contribute My Own Study Notes to the Platform?

    Yes yes...Ofcourse. Just mail them to any one of the team member with Subject as "Chapter name" notes or "Sem Subject Chapter Number" notes along with your name, year and branch. Proper credits will be given to each submission.

  • How Often Are the Study Materials Updated and Expanded?

    Jaise hi hame submission milte hai ham turant hi upload krne ki koshish krte hai. Aur jo hamare taraf se notes hai wo jaise hi class me chapter khtm hota hai uske 3-4 din baad upload ho jate hai.

  • Do I Need to Pay to Access Study Materials on Your Website?

    Nahi. It's completely free but if you want to help us you can always contact us for the same.
